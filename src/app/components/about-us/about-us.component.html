About us

Asalat Al-Khafji offers facility management and skilled manpower services.

Asalat Al-Khafji provides integrated facility management services for residential, commercial, and industrial properties across the Kingdom. By leveraging the latest technologies and mechanisms, Asalat Al-Khafji continuously enhances and develops its services. Their professional team adheres to the highest standards, ensuring that customer expectations are met and that integrated solutions for facilities management are delivered.
In addition, Asalat Al-Khafji supplies skilled manpower to the construction, oil, and gas industries, covering upstream, midstream, and downstream projects and operations. Their highly qualified professionals and skilled workers support a diverse workforce, including Arabs, and Asians (especially Pakistani, Bangladeshi, Nepali, and Indian manpower).
When identifying manpower requirements, Asalat Al-Khafji aligns with customer needs, utilizing screening and matching methods to recommend suitably qualified personnel promptly. They
collaborate with construction & oil and gas companies, facilitating the advancement of their operations with a focus on productivity.
Dependability and the suitability of skilled manpower supply are crucial for operational continuity. Asalat Al-Khafji stands as a reliable partner, ensuring timely responses to customer needs and delivering high-quality services.
